## **Portfolio Recommendations**

* **Core Exposure:** Build foundational positions in integrated leaders like **[Cameco](/company/CCJ.NYSE) (CCJ)** and **[Constellation](/company/CEG.NASDAQ) (CEG)** to capture reliable cash flows and primary supply deficits .  
* **The Midstream Proxy:** Allocate to **[Centrus Energy](/company/LEU.NYSE) (LEU)** and **[BWX Technologies](/company/BWXT.NYSE) (BWXT)** as direct proxies for Western onshoring of critical fuel services and components .  
* **Athabasca Optionality:** Utilize developers like **[NexGen Energy](/company/NXE.NYSE) (NXE)** to gain high-grade leverage to the uranium price without the geopolitical risks of Central Asian production .  
* **Diversified Participation:** Institutional allocators may also use thematic ETFs such as the **[Sprott Uranium Miners ETF](/company/URNM.NYSE) (URNM)** or the **[VanEck Uranium and Nuclear ETF](/company/NLR.NYSE) (NLR)** to manage idiosyncratic risk.
