## **Portfolio Recommendations**

* **Core Exposure:** Build foundational positions in integrated leaders like **Cameco (CCJ)** and **Constellation (CEG)** to capture reliable cash flows and primary supply deficits .  
* **The Midstream Proxy:** Allocate to **Centrus Energy (LEU)** and **BWX Technologies (BWXT)** as direct proxies for Western onshoring of critical fuel services and components .  
* **Athabasca Optionality:** Utilize developers like **NexGen Energy (NXE)** to gain high-grade leverage to the uranium price without the geopolitical risks of Central Asian production .  
* **Diversified Participation:** Institutional allocators may also use thematic ETFs such as the **Sprott Uranium Miners ETF (URNM)** or the **VanEck Uranium and Nuclear ETF (NLR)** to manage idiosyncratic risk.
